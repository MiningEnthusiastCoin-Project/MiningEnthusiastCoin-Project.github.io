About

Bellcoin is a safe and revolutionary crypto currency and is resistant to ASIC and GPU mining.
It adopts a new algorithm "YesPoWer", and adopts "LWMA" as a difficulty algorithm.
It inherits technology based on Bitcoin, and has the most up-to-date features such as SegWit.
Because the block time is as fast as 60 seconds, it works with speedy transactions.
